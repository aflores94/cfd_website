We are Code for Dallas.


A volunteer-led civic technology organization that brings together developers, designers, data enthusiasts, and city staff.

We are an official brigade affiliated with Code for America, whose mission is to harness the digital era to make government work for the people, by the people.

Our focus is on the making the biggest impact on the needs of our community and advocating for open government and open data.
